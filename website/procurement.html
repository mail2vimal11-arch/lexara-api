Procurement Tools

Build Better Language.

Three precision tools for procurement officers and legal counsel — readability linting, McGill citation validation, and a searchable standard clause library. All running locally. No AI quota consumed.

📋 Justice Canada Readability Linter

Flags violations of the Department of Justice's Guide to Readable Contracts — archaic legalese, passive voice, plural party terms ("Bidders must" → "The Bidder must"), and gendered language. Instant, deterministic, no AI required.


📖 McGill Guide Citation Validator

Validates Canadian legal citations against the McGill Guide (10th ed.) — federal and provincial statutes (RSC, SC, SO, SBC…), SOR/SI regulations, and case citations in neutral or law report format. Flags capitalization, format, and style-of-cause issues.


📚 Standard Clause Library

Search and retrieve standardised Canadian procurement clauses — indemnification, limitation of liability, IP ownership, PIPEDA privacy, termination, payment terms, and more. Inspired by the PWGSC SACC Manual, Bonterms, and CommonAccord.
