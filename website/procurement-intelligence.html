📋 Readability Linter

Flags violations of the Department of Justice's Guide to Readable Contracts — legalese, passive voice, plural party terms ("Bidders must" → "The Bidder must"), and gendered language. Includes before/after rewrites for every issue found.

📖 McGill Guide Citation Validator

Validates Canadian legal citations against the McGill Guide (10th ed.) — federal and provincial statutes, SOR/SI regulations, and case citations. Flags capitalization, format, and style-of-cause issues.

📚 Standard Clause Library

Search and retrieve standardised Canadian procurement clauses — indemnification, limitation of liability, IP ownership, PIPEDA privacy, termination, payment terms, and more. Fully deterministic, no AI credits consumed.
